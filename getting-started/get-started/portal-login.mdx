A step-by-step guide to login to Spice.ai and create your first Spice app.

### From the Spice.ai website

1. Go to [spice.ai](https://spice.ai) and click on **Login** or **Try for Free** in the top right corner.

2. You can also navigate directly by URL to [spice.ai/login](https://spice.xyz/login)

1. Click **Continue with GitHub** to grant authorization to login with your GitHub account.

<Frame caption="Login with GitHub">
  <img src="/images/getting-started/get-started/files%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F-Mc71sWdWMqp1e_rsTZE-129938315%252Fuploads%252Fcctg2LkFrZVah0zmtlcR%252FCleanShot%25202023-10-24%2520at%252020.57.15%25402x.png" />
</Frame>

1. Spice now features [Organization Support](/portal/organizations). If you have a pre-existing account, you may be prompted to authorize the Spice AI Platform.

   1. Select and click **Grant** next to all the organizations you would like to use with Spice.

   2. Click **Authorize Spice AI Platform** to grant Spice AI read-only access to your **Organization and Teams** data.
