You can transfer an App's ownership to another [**organization**](/reference/core-concepts/organizations)**.**

App transfer is currently limited to organizations you have access to.

Learn more about [**organization management**](/portal/organizations).

## Transfer an App

1. To transfer an app, click **Settings** in the app navigation.

<Frame caption="App Settings in the portal sidebar.">
  <img src="/images/portal/apps/files%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F-Mc71sWdWMqp1e_rsTZE-129938315%252Fuploads%252FCGmJjaAWK12D7V1tlccH%252FCleanShot%25202023-10-24%2520at%25209%25E2%2580%25AF.08.19%25402x.png" />
</Frame>

1. In the **Danger Zone** section of **App Settings**, click the **Transfer app** button.

![](/images/portal/apps/files%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F-Mc71sWdWMqp1e_rsTZE-129938315%252Fuploads%252Fo4WQvsccfhDlHQWnQqQ8%252FCleanShot%25202023-10-24%2520at%25209%25E2%2580%25AF.10.33%25402x.png)

1. On the **Transfer application** page, select the **New owner** organization from the menu.

![](/images/portal/apps/files%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F-Mc71sWdWMqp1e_rsTZE-129938315%252Fuploads%252FJZy1U86NCBv0PWBw2lex%252FCleanShot%25202023-10-24%2520at%25209%25E2%2580%25AF.11.24%25402x.png)

1. Type the full app name into the text box to confirm and click **Transfer Application** to complete process.

The App will now be accessible by the receiving organization and its members.
